Dr. Dominic Singaraj

Principal Cloud Development Coach | AI Strategist | AWS Advocate

Dr. Dominic Singaraj is a visionary cloud and AI leader, educator, and strategist with over two decades of global experience spanning software development, cloud architecture, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation.

Currently serving as the Principal Cloud Development Coach at Neuefische GmbH, Dominic is the driving force behind empowering the next generation of cloud professionals, particularly through his leadership in the AWS re/Start program. He is also the founder of the AWS One Stop Shop Community, a fast-growing platform focused on democratizing cloud education and accelerating career growth in tech.

Dominic’s AI journey began long before generative AI was a buzzword—dating back to the 1990s, when he first presented on underwater robots searching for pearls. That early spark ignited a lifelong pursuit of intelligent systems, culminating in his completion of the prestigious MIT Executive Program: Artificial Intelligence – Implications for Business Strategy, where he authored a white paper on AI-powered transformation for Fortune 100 companies.

His work spans:

  • Building modern, cloud-native infrastructures on AWS
  • Bridging technical AI knowledge with strategic business value
  • Mentoring aspiring developers, data engineers, and future tech leaders
  • Collaborating with global clients across the US, Germany, UK, India, and beyond

Dr. Singaraj is a regular participant at leading conferences like the World AI Cannes Festival, where he connects with industry pioneers such as Dr. Janet Sherlock (Ralph Lauren), Nia Castelly (Google Checks), Dileep George (DeepMind), and Rahul Pathak (AWS). His thought leadership focuses on responsible AI, cloud cost optimization, data strategy, and the human-centric side of technology.

A tireless educator, Dominic combines technical depth with storytelling, using platforms like LinkedIn, YouTube, and newsletters to simplify complex ideas and spark curiosity in learners of all levels.
